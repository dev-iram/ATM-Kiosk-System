Kiosk Information and Help


This section contains information, related to Modern Bank ATM Kiosk System. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us through our 24/7 customer support landline at 01 495 2738.
General Help

Login

This ATM is intended for the purpose and use of current Modern Bank customers. The system does not allow for new sign ups, it is simply for the use of current bank members.
In order to login, you must input a valid account number and PIN.

Please note: Your account number is the last 8 digits of your IBAN.

Change PIN

This ATM allows customers to change their account pin.
In order to change your PIN, you will be obligated to input your current PIN, for security reasons.
Once you have clicked '>' and your current PIN has been confirmed as valid, you will be able to input a new PIN. Please note: You are required to re-enter your new PIN to ensure both of the PIN's are the same. If the PIN's are not the same, you will be unable to change your PIN until they are.

After your pin has been confirmed, a message will appear on screen to confirm the change. You will then be returned to the main menu.

View/Edit Personal/Banking Details

This ATM allows customers to view their current Personal Details. It also allows customers to amend their information if required. Please note: Only personal details such as Name, DOB, Email, Phone Number and Address can be updated. Customers are also able ot view their banking details such as their Account Number and PIN, which will be visible when the Checkbox has been clicked.

Transfer

This ATM allows for the transfer of funds digitally. Your account number and balance will be present on this screen, and you will be able to select a payee that you would like to transfer funds to. You can select a cash amount that you would like to transfer with a description that will be available on both your transaction history and the payee's.


Please note: You will be unable to transfer funds that exceed your account balance.

Transaction History

This ATM allows customers to view their previous transaction history. This includes information such as the date when the transaction was made, the time of the transaction, the amount that has been debited/creditted to the account, a description of the transaction, as well as a payee name, if applicable. You will be able to view all of your transaction history within the last three months here.

Session Timeout


After an elapsed time of inactivity, the kiosk system will automatically trigger a session timeout. This is due to the customer not engaging with the program after a certain amount of time - and for the security of the customer to ensure their information is kept safe and secure.

In order to re-access your information, you will be required to login again.
